     Phil 214: Introductory Ethics


     

    Loux Lecture Questions
     

    1) What is ethical relativism and how does it differ from cultural relativism?
    2) Does cultural relativism imply ethical relativism? Explain.
    3) What is Loux's argument against ethical relativism? Explain its premises.
    4) Give a specific example of ethical relativism from our culture, and explain how you would critique this view.
    5) If ethical relativism is implausible, what are the alternatives?
