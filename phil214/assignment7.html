 Phil 214: Introductory Ethics

Assignment #7

 

Watch the film "Extreme Measures."  (Starring Hugh Grant and Gene Hackman)

1)      Describe the main ethical dilemma presented in the movie Extreme Measures.

2)      How would Mill, Kant, Aquinas and Aristotle resolve this dilemma?

3)      Dr. Luthan (Hugh Grant) makes an impassioned speech very near the end of the film that presents his main objection to the practices of Dr. Myrick (Gene Hackman).   What would Mill, Kant, Aquinas and Aristotle be likely to say in response to the point Dr. Luthan (Hugh Grant) makes?

4)   What do you think the best approach to the dilemma of the film is?  Explain.
