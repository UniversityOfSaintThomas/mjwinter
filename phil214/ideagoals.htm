The IDEA form requires that I pick goals for the course that you can use as points of evaluation.  Here they are with some elaboration:

IDEA Form Goals for Phil 214

 

 

Goal 1: Gaining a basic understanding of the subject (e.g., factual knowledge, methods, principles, generalizations and theories)

  • Focus on the theories in the first portion of the course (Relativism, Mill, Kant, Aquinas, Aristotle)
  • Focus on fundamental aspects within 4 main theories:
    1. Mill—Greatest Happiness Principle          
    2. Kant—Categorical Imperative
    3. Aquinas—Natural Inclinations/First Precept
    4. Aristotle—Definition of Happiness/Virtue/Function

 

Goal 11: Learning to analyze and critically evaluate ideas, arguments and points of view.

  • Constant focus on arguments in discussions.  Is the argument sound?  Which premise is false?  Why?
  • Focus critical evaluation of arguments in papers.
