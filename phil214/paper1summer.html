 Phil 214: Introductory Ethics

Paper 1

In the article Moral Saints on p.128-138 of EE, Susan Wolf presents an argument against moral sainthood. Your task in the second paper is to present, explain, and critically evaluate Wolf's argument.

First, read the article carefully to determine what Wolf's main conclusion is. Once you have the main conclusion, use the steps outlined in Section 4 of the logic page to formulate a valid version of the main argument.

Use Wolf's article to provide support for each premise.

In the second section of the paper, provide a brief critique of the argument. Which premise or premises are questionable? Why? Do any ideas from this course count against the premises?
