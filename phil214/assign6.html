     Phil 214: Introductory Ethics

    Assignment #6

    Lying
    Readings:

    • Immanuel Kant on lying: Metaphysics of Morals
    • Thomas Aquinas on lying: Treatise on Law

    Killing
    Readings:

    • Thomas Aquinas on killing: Treatise on Law
       
    Questions:
    1.  Do you find Kant and Aquinas' positions on lying plausible?  Explain.  Is it ever permissible to lie?  Why/why not?

    2. Do you find Aquinas views about killing plausible?  Explain.  Is it ever permissible to intentionally kill an innocent human being?
