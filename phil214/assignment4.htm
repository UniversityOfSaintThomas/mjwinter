 Phil 214: Introductory Ethics

Assignment #4

Reading

Please read the selections from books 1,2, and 3 of Aristotle's Nicomachean Ethics found here.

1) What is the highest good? Why? 
2) Why aren't the lives of pleasure or honor the best? 
3) What are the characteristics of the good? 
4) What is Aristotle's function argument?   Present it in a formally valid pattern. 
5) How does Aristotle divide the soul? How is the division relevant to Aristotle's ethical theory? 
6) How does Aristotle define excellence (virtue)? 
7) How can there be absolute moral standards if the mean is relative? 
8) What kinds of activities have no mean? Why?
