 Phil 214: Introductory Ethics

Paper #1
 

Please carefully read the following article by Peter Singer

The Singer Solution to World Poverty

 

Your assignment is to present, explain and evaluate Singer's main argument.  More specifically, in Section I of your paper, present a formally valid two-premise argument for Singer's main conclusion.   Explain and justify each premise of the argument you present using Singer's article and any other information you take to support his premises.

In Section II of the paper you are to critique the argument presented in from Section I.  Which premise of the argument is false?  Why?  How do ideas discussed in class relate to the premises of the argument?  Present an example of your own that illustrates why a premise is false.  Important: Make sure your example does not assume the point at issue.

The general guidelines and grading criteria for the paper are here:
