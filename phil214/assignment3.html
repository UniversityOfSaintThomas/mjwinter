Questions

Aquinas on natural law

1) When is killing acceptable according to Aquinas? Why? 
2) Does Aquinas think that some actions are always wrong? Why? 
3) Explain Aquinas' principle about actions that have two effects. What role does intention have in the application of this principle? 
4) Is lying ever acceptable according to Aquinas? Why?
