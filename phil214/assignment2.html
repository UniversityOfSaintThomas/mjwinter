Questions

Kant on the categorical imperative
1) What is the difference between hypothetical and categorical imperatives?
2) What is the categorical imperative?
3) What is the practical imperative?
4) What role do motives and intentions play in Kant's theory?

Aquinas on natural law
6) What does it mean to say that "reason is the principle of human actions"?
7) How can one differentiate between eternal, divine, natural and human law?
8) What is the first precept of the natural law?
9) What is the order of natural inclinations?
10) Are all principles of natural law known?  Are they all true?
