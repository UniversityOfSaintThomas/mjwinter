Study Guide for the Final

The exam will cover material from, Searle, Chalmers, and Aristotle/Aquinas. I
expect that you will be familiar with the readings we have addressed from each author. You
may use the questions assigned for each philosopher along with the responses you have
provided to help focus your preparation for the exam.

-Searle's solution to the mind/body problem (ch.1)
-Searle on the refutation of AI. (ch.2)
-Searle on free will/determinism/compatibilism (ch.6)
-Chalmers on materialism/Chalmers' dualism

-Aquinas' modus tollens argument against materialism (Q.75, a.1)
-An argument against hylomorphism along with Aquinas' response (Q.76, a.1, obj.1)
-Arguments about gender and human nature (Assignment #10).
