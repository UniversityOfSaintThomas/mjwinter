Philosophy of the Human Person

Assignment #1

Read the following section of Plato's Phaedo:

1. Prologue (57a-59c)
2. Death and the Philosopher (59c-70c)

Written assignment
Socrates argues for the conclusion that "All philosophers accept death willingly." Construct a formally valid argument for this conclusion. Provide a brief explanation of each of the two premises.
