Syllogisms

Indicate figure, mood and validity.

1) No American president has been completely honest.
   Some lawyer was an American president.
   Some lawyer was not completely honest.

2) All apples are orange.
   Some pears are orange.
   Some pears are apples.

3) All chordates have a heart.
   Some renates have a heart.
   Some renates are not chordates.

4) Every renate has a heart.
   All mammals have a heart.
   All mammals are renates.

5) All humans are mammals.
   Most teachers are humans.
   Most teachers are mammals.
