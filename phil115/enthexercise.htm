Enthymemes

Complete the following to make valid arguments.

1) All mammals are land-dwelling, since all mammals have lungs.

2) Since no mammal has scales, some elephants do not have scales.

3) No mammal has scales, but all elephants are mammals, so...

4) Because some gorillas are in the zoo, some gorillas are in captivity.

5)  All politicians accept campaign funds.  Thus, no politician is completely honest.

6) No free action is constrained.  All voluntary actions are free.  Thus...

7) No machine acts from deliberation.  Every computer is a machine.  So....
