Philosophy of the Human Person

Assignment #3

Read the rest of the Phaedo (sections 9-15). Socrates presents a final argument for the immortality of the soul in sections 12-13. 

Take "The soul is immortal" as the conclusion of the argument (105e-106b) Find as many premises in this argument as possible. For each premise you find, provide a brief explanation.  The argument you hand in should have many premises. It need not be formally valid. I just want you to present and explain different stages in the argument.
