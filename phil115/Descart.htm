Descartes' Meditations
 

Read Descartes' first Meditation.

1) Why does Descartes doubt his senses?

2) How can he possibly doubt that he is sitting by the fire?

3) How can he possibly doubt that 2+3=5?

4) Why does Descartes propose to doubt everything?

Read Meditation 2

1) What is the first thing Descartes knows with certainty?

2) What is a human being essentially?

3) When can we know that something is true?

4) What is the point of the wax example?

Read Meditation 5

1) Set up Descartes'argument that is supposed to prove that God exists. Is it sound?

2) What does the nature of God consist in for Descartes?

3) What is Descartes' basis for saying that something is necessarily true?

Read meditation 6

1) What is the new reason given for doubting the first person statement " I am in pain"?

2) What is Descartes' argument for the separability of the soul?

3) How does Descartes come to terms with his doubt about the sense?

4) How does he come to terms with the dreaming objection?
