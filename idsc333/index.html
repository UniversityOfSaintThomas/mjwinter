Objectives:
The purpose of this course is to grapple with some questions related to thought control and the human condition.  This course will help students develop the ability to reflect philosophically on both practical and theoretical issues involving the nature of thought control and its impact on the lives of human beings. A significant portion of this course will take place through a Discussion Forum on the Internet.
 

Texts:
Manufacturing Consent: The Political Econcomy of the Mass Media: Edward Herman and Noam Chomsky
PR! A Social History of Spin; Stuart Ewen
