Objectives:
The purpose of this course is to examine some aspects of the relationship between technology, propaganda and the human condition.  This course will help students develop the ability to reflect philosophically on both practical and theoretical issues involving the nature of technology and its impact on the lives of human beings. A significant portion of this course will take place through a Discussion Forum on the Internet.
