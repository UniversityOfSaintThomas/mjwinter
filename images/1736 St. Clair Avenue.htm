3BR/2BA Arts & Crafts Bungalow in the Heart of Mac-Groveland

 

Click Here For A Virtual Tour with 360-Degree Panoramas!



This charming 3 bedroom, 1.5 bathroom Arts & Crafts Bungalow is located in the heart of the desirable Macalester-Groveland neighborhood; one block from Macalester College, within a mile of St. Thomas and St. Catherine, and a short commute to the University of Minnesota; conveniently located to grocery stores, restaurants and shops, and direct public transportation to the U and DT Minneapolis and St Paul. It has been recently refinished and updated with new appliances and other amenities, including: washer, dryer, dishwasher, central air & heat, and more.

The main floor has a furnished three-season porch, beautiful dark crafted woodwork, extensive dining room with built-in buffet, refinished hardwood floors, sun-filled updated light maple kitchen, and a dramatic living room with full west wall brick fireplace flanked by glass-doored book cases and window seats. The second floor master suite includes a sky-lit half bath, two additional skylights, custom built-ins and wardrobe, and a cozy reading nook. The basement is clean, well-lit, and offers plenty of additional storage space.

The back yard, a private haven, features two brick paver patios and walk ways, new professionally built 17′x24′ deck, new two car garage, and landscaped low-maintenance gardens. These features make this inviting home a rare blend of old world appointments and modern day convenience.
