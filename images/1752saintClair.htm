1752 St. Clair Avenue

Your new home in the Mac-Groveland neighborhood of St. Paul.

 

 

This charming 3 bedroom, 2 bathroom Bungalow is located in the heart of the desirable Macalester-Groveland neighborhood; one block from Macalester College, within a mile of St. Thomas and St. Catherine, and a short commute to the University of Minnesota; conveniently located to grocery stores, restaurants and shops, and direct public transportation to the U and DT Minneapolis and St Paul. It has been updated with new appliances and other amenities, including: washer, dryer, dishwasher, central air & heat, and more.

The main floor has a front porch, beautiful dark crafted woodwork in the living and dining room, hardwood floors, updated kitchen with oak cabinets, ceramic tile floor and newer appliances.  The full bathroom on the mail level has been completely redone with new fixtures, a glass block window, and a ceramic tile tub surround and floor.  Both bedrooms on the main level have recently refinished hardwood floors and fresh paint.

 

The second floor master suite, which is nearly 350 square feet, includes a half bath with a ceramic tile floor and a roomy maple storage cabinet.   A spacious walk-in closet with oak doors sits next to the bathroom.

 

The basement is partially finished, with an office and a den.  The den has features a built in for a television.  Both the den and the office have new windows, fresh paint, ceramic tile floors, and oak trim and doors.  The remainder of the basement is used for laundry and additional storage space.  The basement windows have been updated with glass block.

The back yard is private with a round brick paver patio.  The yard is closed in by a chain link fence.  An additional 6-foot wooden fence to the west, a tall rose hedge on the alley side, and wall of trees on to the east and a 1.5 car garage on the back corner of the lot make the back yard a private haven within the city.  The front yard is bordered by a tall hedge to the west and a rose hedge to the north.  

 

Two new small cedar decks with stairways to each entry, a fresh coat of exterior paint to the house and garage round out this attractive package.  These features make this inviting home a rare blend of older charm and modern day convenience.
