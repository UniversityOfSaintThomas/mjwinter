Grading Criteria for Ethics Presentations

 

 

 

____ 10 points:  The handout was clear and thorough.

 

____ 10 points:  The argument presented captured the main argument of the article.

 

____ 10 points:  The presenter made adequate reference to specific parts of the article.

 

____ 10 points:  The argument presented captured the complexity of the argument from the article.

 

____ 10 points:  The explanation of each premise was clear and focused.

 

____ 10 points:  Good reasons were given for questioning at least one premise.

 

____ 10 points:  Creative examples were used to illustrate key points.

 

____ 10 points:  Counter-arguments were offered against key premises.

 

____ 10 points:  The presenter responded to questions well.

 

____ 10 points:  The presentation engaged the audience.
