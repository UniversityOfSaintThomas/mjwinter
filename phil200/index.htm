Course Description:

This course will focus on some of the great ideas considered by the ancient Greeks. We will begin by briefly examine some of the most intriguing arguments from the Presocratics, but will focus primarily on the ideas of Plato and Aristotle. Our emphasis throughout the term will be on understanding, analyzing, and evaluating the arguments of the philosophers.  
    

Texts: 

  • Readings in Ancient Greek Philosophy; (Cohen, Curd, Reeve) 

  Grading:
